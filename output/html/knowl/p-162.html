The only tools you and your fellow prisoners have to aid you are random number generators, which will give each prisoner a random number, uniformly and independently chosen between zero and one (i.e. you could get .25 or .64, etc on this number generator). These random number generators may be a helpful tool in making individual decisions about whether to play or pass. As part of your strategy, though you cannot decide beforehand who will play and who will pass, you can decide a number from the random number generator that will be a threshold below which a prisoner will play, and above which they will pass.
