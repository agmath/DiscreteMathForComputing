In the final two chapters of our course we encountered arrays and got a quick introduction to graph theory. In our chapter on arrays we encountered both one-dimensional arrays (vectors), and multidimensional arrays (matrices and tensors). In our chapter on graph theory, we developed the notion of a graph as a network of nodes, potentially connected via edges. We saw that graphs find many uses in computing. They serve as models for everything from database design, to file systems, or social networks.
